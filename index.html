Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Chicken Dip

By: Frank's Red Hot

Makes:
4 cups
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
20 minutes

This delicious buffalo chicken dip from Frank's Red Hot is easy and fun to make for any type of party!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 8oz. package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup of FRANK'S RedHot Original Cayenne Pepper Sauce or FRANK'S RedHot Buffalo Wings Sauce
  • 1/2 cup of ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup of blue cheese crumbles

Directions:

  1. PREHEAT over for 350 . Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Spoon into shallow 1-quart baking dish.
  2. BAKE for 20 minutes or until it is heated through; stir. Sprinkle with green onions.
  3. If desired, serve with chips, crackers, and/or veggies.
